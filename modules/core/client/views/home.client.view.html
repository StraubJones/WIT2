What would you do . . . ?

Ever wonder if you would know the right thing to say if a coworker says something sexist?

This app is here to:

  • Help you think through potential scenarios beforehand to avoid the deer-in-headlights effect.
  • Weigh the pros and cons of different types of responses
  • And be fully prepared the next time you want to stand up for the women around you.
